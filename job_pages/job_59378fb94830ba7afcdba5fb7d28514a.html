Position Overview:The Enterprise Data Architect focuses on information architecture and is a leader for enterprise information design and development. This role sets enterprise architecture information standards, guidelines and principles across the organization. Additionally, the enterprise architect defines delivery architecture patterns and methods to support the enterprise’s information consumption needs. The role is also responsible for leading the organization and architecture community around emerging trends in the information space.
