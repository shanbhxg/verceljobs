Job Description The Opportunity We at Flywire are seeking a Senior People Data and Reporting Analyst. The People Senior Data and Reporting Analyst performs complex analytics and statistical modelling on large data sets that identify current People sector trends and information patterns. This job is responsible for examining and identifying data patterns and trends that help answer business questions and improve decision-making. The Senior Data Analyst job is responsible for collecting data sources analyzing and extracting key data and information. This job also evaluates and monitors data quality to meet the organization's information system needs and requirements.
