Overview: The Vice President, Project Management Office (PMO) Governance role provides project management governance across the enterprise. This includes leadership over enterprise project management standards and compliance with FDIC and Internal Audit requirements as well as providing leadership to the directors of program offices within business units. This includes the development, execution, and monitoring of Key Performance Indicators (KPI) and Key Risk Indicators (KRI). The role requires strong corporate governance and project management skills and thorough understanding of the role and function of a project management office in a regulating financial institution.
