Full Time | Columbia, MD

We are looking for an enthusiastic FAA Technical Trainer with 5+ years experience to develop training material and conduct training classes for customer personnel on topics related to the operation and maintenance of digital and analog voice and data telecommunication products. International travel is required. Military background a plus. Adhere to contractual format, content, and style guidelines. Produce training schedules, classroom agendas, and course content according to program objectives. Conduct on-site customer training when required, making all necessary arrangements. Maintain and report data on completed courses. Evaluate results of training programs. Determine overall effectiveness of training program and make improvements.
