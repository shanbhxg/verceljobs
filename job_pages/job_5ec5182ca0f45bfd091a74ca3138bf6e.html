Harris County Courts at Law – Office of Court Management The Office of Court Management provides administrative and technical support for the County Criminal(16), County Civil(4), and Justice of the Peace Courts(16) in Harris County, Texas. The Office of Court Management Technical Team assists the courts by providing reports to help manage the court dockets, maintaining the computer hardware and software, preparing statistical analysis of caseload trends and developing a data warehouse with a court dashboard for the criminal courts. We provide the public face of the courts through the development and maintenance of the Harris County Courts’ websites.
