Our Product Management group has a new position available for a Documentation Specialist / Release Coordinator. This individual will organize and produce Beta, Gamma and version documentation prior to rollout. Will also write release feature descriptions and notifications as well as integrating these into our help system and corporate wiki. Additional responsibilities include documenting all customer questions, feedback, bug reports and intradepartmental release and hot fix related communications that come to Product Management.
