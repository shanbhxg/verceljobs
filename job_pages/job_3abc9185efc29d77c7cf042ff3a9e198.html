As a Management Consulting Senior Associate at RSM, you will work with mid-sized businesses in diverse industries and receive hands-on client experience with direct access to executive management. RSM's Management Consulting Group provides strategy and advisory assistance to help companies increase their overall efficiencies, effectiveness, and improve the IT alignment with the business functions and processes.
