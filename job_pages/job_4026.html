About the job The Technical Writer partners with internal stakeholders to understand and logically document current and future processes. The Technical Writer evaluates and revises the current processes and ensures that the logical flows make sense. Additionally the Writer researches best practices around new writing methods so that they can improve the maturity of new and existing documentation.
