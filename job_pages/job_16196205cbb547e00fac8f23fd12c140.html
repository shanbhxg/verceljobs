Reporting to the Manager of End User Support, this position is the onsite technical lead and is responsible for monitoring that standards, processes and policies are being followed by End User Support Analysts in order to ensure support is delivered in a consistent and timely fashion, and in line with IS mission and objectives. Lead End User Analysts are responsible to oversee and assist in the installation and troubleshooting of our end user computing environment, both hardware and software. Lead’s will distribute workload, provide and escalation point for issues, give direction and mentor staff.
