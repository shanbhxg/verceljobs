Support users both in person and by phone. Assorted project work including maintaining and implementing patch management, inventory management, imaging, workstation backup systems, and software deployments. Desktop workstation setups and imaging. Write internal and user-facing documentation. Required Qualifications:A strong methodical approach to troubleshooting technology issues. Deep experience working with and troubleshooting Microsoft Office. Strong communication skills both written and spoken. Top notch people skills and a positive customer service attitude. At least 2 years demonstrated experience supporting computers in an enterprise environment.
