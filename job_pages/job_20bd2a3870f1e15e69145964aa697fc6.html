POSITION SUMMARYThe incumbent in this position will work closely with a team of business systems analysts to design, implement and maintain business applications data automation processes. The incumbent must be familiar with at least one SDLC process (e. g., AGILE, SCRUM, Unified Process, Waterfall) and be able to apply that process to their job function. The individual will design and create object-oriented solutions, using the Microsoft .NET Framework. The individual will design and create SQL stored procedures and user-defined functions. The individual will author functional and technical specification documents. The individual will serve as an SME for the common codebase, providing direct support where they are most familiar, and continually learning and adopting where they are least familiar.
