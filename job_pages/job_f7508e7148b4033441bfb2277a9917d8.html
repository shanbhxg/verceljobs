Amazon's Builder Tools team is hiring a technical program manager to lead a project that will redefine how we release software. The Senior TPM role requires excellent leadership skills, strong technical expertise and hands-on project management skills. The Builder Tools org provides the tools and services that enable Amazon and AWS customer to maintain its pace of innovation.
