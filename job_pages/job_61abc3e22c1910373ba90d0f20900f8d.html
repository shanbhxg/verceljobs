Information Security/Cybersecurity Analyst The New Jersey Judiciary has an exciting opportunity for an experienced technical professional to lead efforts in the development, deployment, and support of enterprise security strategies that protect the infrastructure and information assets of the courts. This innovative and analytical expert will work in a large scale n-tier computing environment that utilizes technologies including IBM i SAM, Data Power, LDAP, Active Directory, RACF, Unix, and Web Services Security in support of SOA. Key responsibilities will include:Administer RACF security Establish and maintain standards and best practices for mainframe security users, groups, resources and system configuration Streamline security processes, utilizing programs and available tools. Research emerging threats and responses Collaborate on solutions design, and evaluate cybersecurity tools that safeguard the integrity of cross-platform services including mobile, webbased, and mainframe applications Participate in the development and enforcement of information security standards and policies Perform security audits and assessments including PCI DSS, DLP, network penetration, web application scans, and pen tests On-call weekend and non-business hour production / emergency support may be required. Applicants who do not possess the required education may substitute additional paraprofessional and/or professional IT technical experience on a year for year basis with one (1) year of such experience being equal to thirty (30) semester hour credits.
