Responsibilities:• : Manages a team of IT systems analysts to ensure end-users receive the highest level of customer service

• Develop, define and communicate methods for trending end-user problems and issues to ensure appropriate action is taken by the correct group to ensure resolution/improvement
• Develop, define, communicate and track methods of measuring end-user satisfaction
• Consistently model the highest levels of support orientation and professionalism
• Assist in hiring, termination and performance reviews and disciplinary action for all IT Helpdesk staff
• Manages level 1 IT support to the end-user community
