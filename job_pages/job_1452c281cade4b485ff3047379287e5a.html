Position Description Overall Purpose of PMO Analyst Role: The role of Project Management Office Analyst is to support the Project Management Office, ensuring that client solutions are delivered on time, within scope, to budget and to the required quality standards. The PMO analyst provides analytical support and reporting to the Project Manager and technical team as directed to PM. The PMO analyst is responsible for ensuring the scope project plan is communicated to all project stakeholders together with individual responsibilities. The PMO analyst will also assisting the Project Manager with defining and updating the project management processes, standards and governance, and coordinate activities in support of quality objectives PMO Analyst
