As an Industrialization (JAAMS System) Engineer, you will provide development efforts around JAAMS technology stack for the US Perso Centers – currently Atlanta, Jacksonville and Rhode Island. Provide architecture design documents around tools and technologies within scope. Develop programs and components using best practices and methodologies. Provide testing plans and validation plans against development efforts.
