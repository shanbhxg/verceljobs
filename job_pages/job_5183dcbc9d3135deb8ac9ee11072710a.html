Role Overview Application Analysts produce accurate and comprehensive project documentation and other deadline deliverables, and assist with various tasks throughout the development life cycle of a project. They are a support resource to the customer while participating as a member of the project teams. Application Analysts are typically responsible for the tasks associated with a single phase on a project or general tasks throughout all phases of a single project.
