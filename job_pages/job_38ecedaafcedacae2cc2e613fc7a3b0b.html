The Support Technician II position is a combination of telecom/IT support and data center operations. IT This person is responsible for technical customer support that handles requests to install, troubleshoot, and maintain voice and data equipment and cabling including voice/data circuit cut-overs during maintenance windows. The candidate will also perform walk throughs of the data centers as well as the facility's common areas, respond to data center alarms, monitor security cameras, and assist in other various projects that are assigned by the General Manager.
