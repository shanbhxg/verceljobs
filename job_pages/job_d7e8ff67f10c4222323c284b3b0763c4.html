Full Time | Irving, TX

Additional Information:
Northrop Grumman Enterprise Shared Services is seeking an Industrial Security Assistant to join our team of qualified, diverse individuals. This position will be located in Irving, TX. The qualified candidate will be responsible for Sensitive Compartmentalized Information (SCI) Personnel Security Access Processing through multiple Northrop Grumman IC customer partners. The candidate must be an experienced security professional with strong leadership, problem solving, and interpersonal skills. They must be able to adapt in a highly charged and challenging work environment. They will be primarily responsible for the receipt and dispatch of personal information, classified and unclassified material, personnel security, customer service, process improvement, security education and development.
