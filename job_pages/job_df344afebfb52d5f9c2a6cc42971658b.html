Additional Information:
The Technical Project Lead (TPL) effectively leads, coordinates and oversees work across IT projects within a department and/or across departments or with a 3rd Party. In this capacity, the incumbent assumes responsibility for detailed technical project management tasks and activities from original concept through final implementation. The Technical Project Lead collaborates with stakeholders to formulate project scope for the management of large projects or several smaller independent projects. The technical project lead may also get involved in some of the business requirements analysis tasks and participate in requirements gathering related to screening new demand requests. The incumbent manages all aspects of project, from beginning to end, with direct accountability for project execution while leading a team, or teams, to accomplish specific objectives in a given period with available resources and within defined budgets. The Technical Project Lead is responsible for reporting status, risks, and issues to higher-level management. Additionally, The Technical Project Lead collaborates with internal groups and external vendors to assure the timely completion of all scheduled tasks.
