About the Job The Office of Information Technology (OIT) at the University of Minnesota is looking for a business/systems analyst to join the Student Administration Services team. Student Administration Services manages the systems that support administrative business processes throughout the student lifecycle. Functions include registration, tuition and billing, financial aid, and degree awarding (among others). The person in this position will be responsible for business process analysis (with a systems focus), design, implementation, and testing for a broad range of systems. The successful candidate will be an experienced professional with excellent communication and collaboration skills, the ability to learn our systems quickly, and prior experience managing small to medium-sized projects.
