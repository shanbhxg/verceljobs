Senior Engineering Project Manager As a member of the Home Monitoring and Automation engineering team, you will be responsible for managing the development of video surveillance cameras, home monitoring/automation gateways, and proximity sensors for service providers as well as for worldwide retail channels. These products will incorporate most of the following technologies: 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac Wireless, Zigbee, Zwave, Blutooth LE, Ethernet, DNS/DHCP, IP, HTTP, SNMP, and embedded Linux. The primary function of an Engineering Project Manager is to work with product marketing and ODM coordinating and finalizing the product requirements in functionality, cost and schedule. The Engineering Project Manager manages ODM and coordinate with various in-house engineering team to ensure that high-quality products are delivered in a timely manner and in accordance with the company procedures and systems. In addition the engineer oversees the activities between the component suppliers and the ODM. The engineer must manage the technical issues that arise during the approval process and ensure rapid resolution by working with and through the sales engineers, ODMs and Component vendors. Job
