Our perfect candidate would have a strong technical background that may have previously developed themselves and eventually became a Project Manager. Great at knocking down roadblocks. You are a driver, a leader, a self-starter, self-motivated, self-directed and self-sufficient, at the same time you are very team oriented. Experienced and savvy enough to work with external vendors, internal departments, and partners. Assertive with a strong knowledge of and experience with iterative and agile development methodologies. Must be able to ensure concepts and principles are adhered to, must be the voice of reason, and must be able to work with your team to make tough calls when necessary. Exceptional communications skills, written as well as oral, are an absolute must.
