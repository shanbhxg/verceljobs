Description Cox Communications is looking for a Lead Technical Analyst who will collaborate with the Infrastructure design and support teams to determine application, database and other systems capacity demands and impacts based on evolving requirements and trends. The Technical Analyst works closely with other members of the technology teams to ensure the right resources are available at the right time to meet the capacity needs of the business. Additional responsibilities will include working with the same technical teams to establish technology roadmaps as well as maintaining asset inventories for the purposes of lifecycle and capacity management.
